NP Membership

  • Who It's For: Non-profit organizations seeking to join and benefit from our services without any financial burden.
  • Fee: No fee required.
  • Benefits:
  • Access to a network of like-minded organizations.
  • Free entry to webinars and workshops tailored for non-profit challenges.
  • Opportunities for partnerships and collaborations.
  • Limited access to resources and tools designed for non-profit success.

Bronze Membership

  • Who It's For: Individuals or small businesses starting their journey.
  • Fee: A modest annual fee that provides access to foundational resources.
  • Benefits:
  • Access to basic online courses and webinars.
  • Subscription to a monthly newsletter.
  • Network opportunities with other Bronze members.
  • Discounts on selected partner services.

Silver Membership

  • Who It's For: Established professionals or businesses looking to expand their network and knowledge.
  • Fee: A competitive annual fee for extended benefits and resources.
  • Benefits:
  • All Bronze benefits.
  • Access to advanced training sessions and exclusive webinars.
  • Priority booking for events and conferences.
  • Increased visibility within the member directory.
  • Access to a dedicated support team.

Gold Membership

  • Who It's For: Industry leaders and large corporations committed to excellence and innovation.
  • Fee: A premium annual fee for top-tier services and opportunities.
  • Benefits:
  • All Silver benefits.
  • Exclusive invites to round-table discussions and think tanks with industry leaders.
  • Opportunities for featured articles and promotions in newsletters and on the website.
  • First access to market research reports and case studies.
  • Personalized consulting sessions with experts in your field.
