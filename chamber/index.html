This week, a Martial Arts gym offering to teach Jeet Kune Do and Inosanto Kali was opened.

Jeet Kune Do

Jeet Kune Do (JKD) was developed by the renowned martial artist Bruce Lee. It is not a traditional martial art but rather a philosophy and system that emphasizes simplicity, directness, and efficiency in combat.



Inosanto Kali

Inosanto Kali, developed by Guro Dan Inosanto, is distinct from other martial arts due to its unique combination of Filipino martial arts, Indonesian pencak silat, Western boxing, and various other influences.
