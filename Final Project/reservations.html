Rental Agreement Information

Please read the following rental agreement carefully before proceeding with your reservation:



  1. Rental Period and Rates

    Rental rates are based on the selected rental period (hourly or daily). Rentals must be returned by the due date and time specified in the reservation. Late returns will be subject to additional fees.



  2. Rental Requirements

    All renters must be at least 18 years of age and possess a valid driver's license. Renters may be required to provide proof of insurance and a security deposit.



  3. Damage and Liability

    The renter is responsible for any damage to the rental vehicle during the rental period. Scoots is not liable for any personal injury or property damage resulting from the use of the rental vehicle.



  4. Cancellations and Refunds

    Reservations can be cancelled up to 24 hours before the scheduled rental period for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours of the rental period will be subject to a cancellation fee.



  5. Prohibited Activities

    Rental vehicles may not be used for illegal activities or off-road driving. Smoking and transporting pets are strictly prohibited in the rental vehicles.



By submitting the reservation form, you agree to the terms and conditions outlined in this rental agreement.
