Honda Metro Scooter

The Honda Metro Scooter, known for its fuel efficiency and easy maneuverability, is an ideal choice for short, urban commutes. It typically features a 49cc engine, which means it doesn't require a motorcycle license in many areas. Its compact size allows for easy parking and navigation through city traffic.



Honda Dio Scooter

The Honda Dio is a popular scooter, especially among younger riders, due to its sporty design and reliable performance. It is equipped with a 109cc engine, providing a bit more power than the Metro. The Dio also offers excellent fuel efficiency and a comfortable ride for city commuting.



Honda PCX150 Scooter

The Honda PCX150 is a more powerful and spacious scooter option, perfect for longer commutes or rides with a passenger. It features a 149cc engine, offering a smooth ride and the ability to keep up with faster traffic. It also includes modern amenities like LED lighting and a spacious under-seat storage compartment.



Honda Pioneer ATV

The Honda Pioneer ATV is designed for off-road adventures, equipped with a rugged frame, high-performance suspension, and the capability to handle a variety of terrains. It's available in several models, including options for two to six seats, making it a versatile choice for both work and recreation.



Jeep Wrangler - 4 door with a/c

The four-door Jeep Wrangler with air conditioning combines classic Jeep ruggedness with modern comfort. It’s designed for both off-road trails and city driving, offering ample space for passengers and cargo. The A/C ensures comfort during hot weather, and the four-door model provides easier access and more interior space compared to its two-door counterpart.



Jeep Wrangler - 2 door

The two-door Jeep Wrangler maintains the iconic design and off-road capability the brand is known for, in a more compact package. This model is perfect for drivers looking for a classic Jeep experience with the ability to navigate tight trails and city parking. The two-door model offers less space than the four-door but provides a more traditional Jeep driving experience.
